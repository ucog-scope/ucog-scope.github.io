UCOG-AA scope is designed to help the shooter compensate for the altitude, range and airspeed of the target aircraft (UAV/Drone). It works much like the modern mil-dot reticle with each circle representing 10 m/s aircraft speed. Such scope makes it easier to lead the target when shooting flying aircraft.

Ballistic Calculrator

This is a smart ballistic calculator for long-range and anti-aircraft shooting. It helps shooters calculate lead correction and scope settings necessary for long-range shots. Supports AK and AR families rifles. Available for iPhone, iPad, iPod and Apple Watch. This calculator uses temperature, altitude, humidity, atmospheric pressure, distance to a target, target speed and direction, coriolis effect, and shooting angle for calculating optimal vertical, horizontal and lead corrections.

Smart Electronic Gunsight

Smart electronic sight IOT attachments for aiming Heavy Machine Guns like M2 Browning with tripods, Mortairs, Artillery and other security instruments using ballistic data with the communication with a tactical tablet mobile App (Android, iOS) via Bluetooth 5.0 or PC via USB type-C.

eUCOG finds the magnetic north and any aiming direction in seconds. The system is easily fixed to the instruments through a dovetail, picatinny rail wiht Ammerican Defence Quick Detach(QD), barrel clamp-ons. The size and weight of the system is smaller than the traditional sight units. Can be combined with the traditional sight as an anditional tool.

The aiming data can be entered into the system manually and automatically from a fire control system "Kropiva" or other. "Kropiva" is Ukrainian combat control system for calculating artillery and combat control.

eUCOG aiming is not dependent on visibility conditions. It can be aimed regardless of fog, rain, night, dust, forest, or any other aiming reference limitations.

The user easily adjusts the bipod hand-cranks looking at a graphical scheme. In few seconds, the instrument is ready to fire. The after-firing adjustment is obtained extremely fast, because with eUCOG is ready to fire accurately in under 5 seconds.

Sight can have a backup red dot for for aiming visible targets during the day and night and has laser range finder capabilities.
