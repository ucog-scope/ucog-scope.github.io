Ukrainian Combat Optical Gunsight (UCOG) is a Fight For Ukraine and Yehor Shytikov Project. Fight For Ukraine LLC is the centralized, organized conduit for U.S. and select partner nation private-sector military assistance to Ukraine in its battle with Russia.

This project is U.S. private-sector response to date has been ad hoc and haphazard. UCOG and Fight For Ukraine team employs senior retired and former service members who know how to add focus, clarity, purpose, and organization to the private-sector effort to support Ukraine against a tyrannical foreign invader.

The problem

Rifle scopes have been becoming popular in US army for the right reasons. Scopes on top of a service rifle provide bunch of benefits, some of which include the pleasure of getting higher accuracy, longer range, psychological and personal contentment, and the ability to fire shots from a good distance. The number of pros outweigh the number of cons and makes rifle scopes a must-have equipment for any soldier.

The clock is ticking. We designing and manafucturing optical gunsight for Ukraine, and they save lives. We also need to produce and deliver the AK-74 dovetail and Picatinny rail mounting kit, flashiders and silencers.

SCOPES SAVE LIVES — Ukrainin soldiers can shoot straighter from farther away!

Why Ukrainian Guys also need our 100% titanium silencers: to reduce environmental noise, reduce recoil, eliminate the muzzle flash visible in the dark, mask the location of the soldiers, titanium silencers are lightweight and durable, full-auto rated.
So, our guys can make more precise shots until spoted. Enemy can't hear silenced shooting noise at 200 meter. Our Optic and Silencer is a good combination.

How We Help

We are designing, producing and delivering optical scopes, thermal and night vision devices (in collaboration with AGM) and other critical aid to support Ukrainians at this critical time.

Delivering Humanitarian Aid

This includes food, medical equipment, first aid kids, clothing and boots, headlamps, sleeping bags, temporary shelters, and fire fighting equipment.

Delivering Supplies to Citizens Protecting Their Territory

Essentials like fire fighting equipment, food, and medical support are making their way to citizens defending their homes, cities, and country on the front line.

Supporting the Safe Movement of Refugees

For over 10 million displaced Ukrainians, safe transportation for their family and belongings is the difference between escaping a war zone or living in danger.

Who we are

Fight For Ukraine LLC is designed for one purpose—support Ukraine in its life-or-death battle. We pledge openness and low overheads to focus on programs.

AID For Ukraine

We want to do as much as we can, as fast we can. We know this is possible because of the success of our concurrent initiative. We have delivered:

  • 4 charter planes full of supplies straight to areas in need in Ukraine
  • 68 tonnes (68,000 kilograms) of supplies
  • Fire rescue equipment
  • Medical equipment such as x-ray machines, infant incubators, and hospital beds
  • Warm clothing, jackets, and boots
  • Food and other essentials
  • More than 300 different scopes
  • More than 200 silencers and AK(74/47) optic mounting kits were produced locally in Ukraine
  • Dozen UAV/drones delivered
  • Electronic reconnaissance and warfare equipment
