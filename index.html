UCOG is a Fight For Ukraine Project. Fight For Ukraine LLC project is the centralized, organized conduit for U.S. and select partner nation private-sector military assistance to Ukraine in its battle with Russia.

War is chaos. The U.S. private-sector response to date has been ad hoc and haphazard. Fight For Ukraine employs senior retired and former service members who know how to add focus, clarity, purpose, and organization to the private-sector effort to support Ukraine against a tyrannical foreign invader.

“The fight is on — the need is now.” – Mark Kilbane, CEO

This is all made possible because of individuals and businesses sharing our hope for peace in Ukraine.

The problem

The clock is ticking. We designing and manafucturing optical gunsight for Ukraine, and they save lives. We also need to produce and deliver the AK-74 dovetail and Picatinny rail mounting kit, flashiders and silencers.

What we do

SCOPES SAVE LIVES — Ukrainin soldiers can shoot straighter from farther away!

We work with an extended network of concerned individuals and businesses to raise funds that go directly to the purchase of the heavy-duty, reliable 4x4 pick-up trucks out there. These vehicles are then mobilized immediately to Ukraine’s land borders. Then they’re loaded up with humanitarian supplies, and sent into the country, directly benefiting the people of Ukraine.

How We Help

We are designin, producing and delivery optical scopes and other aid to support Ukrainians at this critical time.

Delivering Humanitarian Aid

This includes food, medical equipment, first aid kids, clothing and boots, headlamps, sleeping bags, temporary shelters, and fire fighting equipment.

Delivering Supplies to Citizens Protecting Their Territory

Essentials like fire fighting equipment, food, and medical support are making their way to citizens defending their homes, cities, and country on the front line.

Supporting the Safe Movement of Refugees

For over 10 million displaced Ukrainians, safe transportation for their family and belongings is the difference between escaping a war zone or living in danger.

Who we are

Fight For Ukraine LLC is designed for one purpose—support Ukraine in its life-or-death battle. We pledge openness and low overheads to focus on programs.

AID For Ukraine

We want to do as much as we can, as fast we can. We know this is possible because of the success of our concurrent initiative – Plane for Ukraine. Plane for Ukraine has delivered:

  • 4 charter planes full of supplies straight to areas in need in Ukraine
  • 68 tonnes (68,000 kilograms) of supplies
  • Fire rescue equipment
  • Medical equipment such as x-ray machines, infant incubators, and hospital beds
  • Warm clothing, jackets, and boots
  • Food and other essentials
